Introduction

Large magical reptile-like creatures of immense strength, each infused with a element, from which they draw their power. There are four known types of dragons:

  • Air dragons: Spawns at high attitudes, always flying, levitation effect on attacks;
  • Fire dragons: Spawns on ground, can fly, fire breath;
  • Water Dragons: Spawns on water, can swim, water breath;
  • Earth dragons: Spawns on ground, binding mud breath, physically strongest;
